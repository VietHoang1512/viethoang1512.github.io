My name is Hoang Phan, I graduated from the honors program at [School of Information and Communication Technology (SoICT)](https://soict.hust.edu.vn/),  [Hanoi University of Science & Technology (HUST)](https://www.hust.edu.vn/) with a Computer Science Bachelor's degree.

Beside, I'm currently a Research Resident at [VinAI Research](https://www.vinai.io/), Machine Learning group, under the supervision of [Prof. Trung Le](https://research.monash.edu/en/persons/trung-le) and [Prof. Nhat Ho](https://nhatptnk8912.github.io/). Before joining VinAI, I was a research student at [Data Science Laboratory](http://ds.soict.hust.edu.vn/), advised by [Dr. Linh Ngo](https://users.soict.hust.edu.vn/linhnv/) and [Prof. Khoat Than](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=z2_6ZRYAAAAJ&hl).

My research goal is to build machine learning agents that can mimic the learning process of humans. To fulfill this goal, I have been working on a number of topics: continual learning, domain adaptation and generalization, and multitask learning.  Currently, my research primarily focuses on multi-task learning.
